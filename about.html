About Me

I am a recent Cambridge graduate, enthusiastic about tutoring a wide range of subjects from GCSEs and A Levels to university admissions exams and interviews. I received all grade 9s and A*s, and have experience in tutoring on university admission.

My qualifications include a Bachelor of Arts in Natural Sciences from the University of Cambridge, where I specialized in Physics, Mathematics, Biology, and other sciences. I achieved straight A*s in A level Physics, Mathematics, Biology, and in an extended project qualification on healthcare systems. At GCSE level, I attained all grade 9s in subjects including Physics, Biology, Chemistry, Mathematics, English Language, English Literature, Spanish, Religious Studies, Design and Technology, and Food Technology.
